# Day 1: Upper Body Strength

## Warm-up (10 min)

- Arm circles
- Jumping jacks
- High knees
- Mountain climbers

## Main Workout (70 min)

### Strength Circuit (3 rounds, 40 sec work, 20 sec rest)

- Push-ups
- Pull-ups
- Dips
- Pike push-ups
- Inverted rows
- Bicep curls
- Russian twists
- Ab wheel rollouts
- Superman

### Cardio

- 15 min rowing
- 15 min jump rope

### Balance

- Single-leg stand: 3 sets of 30 seconds each leg

## Cool-down and Stretching (10 min)

- Standing forward bend
- Child's pose
- Doorway chest stretch
